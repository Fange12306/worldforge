# Human Federation

The largest unified government of the interstellar era, a federal representative democracy composed of 87 member planets. The Federation Parliament sits in New Geneva on New Earth, with seats allocated by population.

During its expansion period (220–245), the Federation achieved remarkable feats — establishing a trade network spanning hundreds of light-years, creating a unified interstellar legal system, and building a formidable Federal Fleet. But the Warp Ban exposed the fundamental tensions within the Federation's structure: security versus freedom, control versus autonomy, human interests versus the rights of other forms of intelligence.

The Warp Ban passed with a parliamentary majority on paper, but opposition grows louder by the day. Many frontier planets see the Ban as a tool for New Earth to tighten central control. This division sets the stage for what follows.
